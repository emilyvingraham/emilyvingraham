About

Emily V. Ingraham is a Front-End Web Developer based in the United States. Prior to this, Emily lived and worked in New York City for 10 years in the financial services sector of real estate investments. During this time, Emily worked on various projects involving implementation, data migration, end-user testing and project management.

Emily currently lives in Connecticut and has a Bachelor's Degree in Psychology from UConn, a Certificate in Accounting from BMCC, and a Front-End Web Development Certificate from SheCodes.

In her spare time, Emily enjoys reading, learning, hiking, watching the Bravo Network and playing with any dog that is in close proximity.
