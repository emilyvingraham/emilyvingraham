About

Emily Ingraham is a Front-End Web Developer based in the United States. Prior to this, Emily lived and worked in New York City for 10 years in the financial services sector of real estate investments. After working on various projects involving implementation, data migration, end-user testing and project management, Emily made the decision to change her career to the field of technology, focusing specifically on Front-End Web Development.

Emily currently lives in Colorado and has a Bachelor's Degree in Psychology from UConn, a Certificate in Accounting from BMCC, and is in the process of earning a Front-End Web Developer Certificate from SheCodes.

In her spare time, Emily enjoys reading, learning, hiking, watching the Bravo Network and playing with any dog that is in close proximity.
